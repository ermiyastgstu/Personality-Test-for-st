Welcome To Futurepath for Ethiopians Personality test website

This website contains 50 questions that are used to calculate combined personality (MBTI, Ocean and Enneagram).So Please students rate 1 (Strongly disagree) → 5 (Strongly agree) to calculate your personality Examples for students are shown in parentheses.

Before You go to test Do the following to get accurate

  1. Be Calm— Avoid Stress or Strong Emotions like sad, anxious etc... br
  2. Be Alone in a Quiet Place
  3. Answer Based on Your Long-Term Behavior, Not Today. Ask yourself: “How have I usually behaved for the LAST 2–3 years?”
  4. Be Honest — Don’t Pick the ‘Good’ or ‘Ideal’ Answer
  5. Don’t Think Too Much — Choose Your First Natural Reaction
  6. Don’t Take the Test Twice in the Same Day
  7. Avoid External Influences like social Media
  8. Understand That No Person Is 100% One Type
